Core Beliefs

Context

Social marketers and researchers are no longer content simply counting mentions: social becomes a powerful insights tool only when we can understand the context of conversations.

Emotion

Canvs layers the emotional graph on top of the social graph to reveal context behind social conversations. We believe that understanding how audiences emotionally relate to content is the first step in building a meaningful relationship with them.

Nuance

Entertainment content is complex—that’s why we don’t believe in positive, negative, and neutral sentiment. Canvs uncovers nuanced audience emotions and lets you decide what they mean for your show.

Mashwork was established in 2010 by entrepreneur Jared Feldman and Stern School of Business professor, Sam Hui, PhD. We excel in synthesizing data from countless best-in-class tools to deliver actionable insights.

Canvs™, Mashwork’s proprietary social TV analytics platform, provides scalable measurement and qualitative analysis of Twitter reactions to entertainment content. Our technology is based on years of R&D, and was tested in private beta with top content providers like Viacom and Time Warner.
